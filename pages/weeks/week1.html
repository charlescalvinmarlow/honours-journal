Something Material Honours Journal

Week 1

Identify Interests

Using my thoughts and notes from readings and recent classes I went through the things I found interesting and just put them on paper.

I grouped the different pieces by a few different types: values, skills, bases, topics and personal. In order to organise them I wanted to try and make something interactive. I made a quick prototype in Figma.

The process of creating this helped to consider how some of these things are linked, but also the limitation of the layout I chose, the topics can also be linked, there could be directional links that flow through one element to another, and would be interesting to explore in the next time I need to generate ideas.
